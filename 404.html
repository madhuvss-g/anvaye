General
Anvaye is an Indian e-commerce platform that connects buyers with verified Instagram sellers. Buyers can discover unique handmade and curated products, while sellers can list their products and receive payments directly via UPI. Think of it as a trusted marketplace built specifically for Instagram sellers and their customers.
Yes! Anvaye is available across all of India. Any buyer or seller with an Indian mobile number can sign up and start using Anvaye immediately.
Currently Anvaye is available in English. Support for Hindi and other regional Indian languages is on our roadmap and coming soon.
You can reach us at anvaye-in@slkjijhk.com. We respond within 24 hours on all days. For urgent issues, Anvaye Pass subscribers get priority support with faster response times.
🛍️
For Buyers
Simply enter your Indian mobile number on the buyer sign-up page and verify with a one-time OTP. No email, no password, no long forms. You're in within 30 seconds.
Anvaye Pass is a subscription for buyers at ₹50 for 15 days. It unlocks access to all sellers on Anvaye, removes per-order platform fees, and gives you priority customer support. Without a pass, you can still browse and order from free sellers at no cost.
Yes! A selection of sellers is available for free browsing and ordering without any subscription. Anvaye Pass simply unlocks the full catalogue of all sellers.
After placing an order, go to your Orders section and click "Track order". You'll see real-time status updates — Confirmed, Packing, Shipped, and Delivered. You'll also receive notifications at each stage.
First, contact the seller directly through Anvaye's messaging system. If the issue is not resolved within 48 hours, contact our support team at anvaye-in@slkjijhk.com and we will mediate on your behalf.
Returns are subject to each seller's individual return policy, which is displayed on their store page. Anvaye encourages all sellers to have fair return policies. Contact the seller directly to initiate a return.
After your order is marked as Delivered, you'll receive a prompt to leave a review. Only buyers who actually purchased can leave reviews — ensuring all ratings are genuine and tamper-proof.

For Sellers
Go to the seller sign-up page, verify your Indian mobile number via OTP, connect your Instagram handle, add your store name and description, then list your first product. The whole process takes under 5 minutes.
Yes. Currently Anvaye is designed for Instagram sellers. Your Instagram handle is your store identity on Anvaye. Support for sellers without Instagram is on our roadmap.
Listing products is completely free. Anvaye charges only 3% per successful sale. There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no setup costs. If you don't sell, you pay nothing.
Payments go directly to your UPI ID at the time of purchase via Razorpay. There is no holding period. Money is in your account within minutes of the buyer paying.
There is no limit on the number of products you can list. Add as many products as you want — completely free.
Your seller dashboard shows all incoming orders in real-time. You can update order status (Packing → Shipped → Delivered), view revenue analytics, and manage your product catalogue all from one place.
Yes! You can use Anvaye as both a buyer and a seller. Simply sign up for both roles using the same mobile number. Switch between buyer and seller modes from your profile.
💸
Payments
All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway used by millions of businesses. Your card and UPI details are never stored on Anvaye's servers. All transactions are encrypted and PCI DSS compliant.
Anvaye supports UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), debit cards, credit cards, and net banking. UPI is the recommended and fastest payment method.
Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis depending on the seller's return policy. If you have a genuine issue with your order, contact our support team and we will work to resolve it fairly for both parties.
No. Unlike most platforms, Anvaye does not hold payments. Money goes directly from the buyer to the seller's UPI ID at the time of purchase. This is one of Anvaye's key differentiators.
📦
Orders
Delivery times vary by seller and product. Each seller specifies their shipping timeline on their product pages. Typical delivery is 3–7 business days for most sellers across India.
You can request a cancellation before the seller marks the order as Shipped. Contact the seller directly through Anvaye's messaging system. Once shipped, cancellation is subject to the seller's return policy.
