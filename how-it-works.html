For Buyers
From sign up to delivery — here's exactly how it works
1
Sign up with your phone number
No email, no password, no long forms. Just enter your Indian mobile number and verify with a one-time OTP. You're in within 30 seconds.
Secured with OTP verification
2
Browse verified sellers
Discover unique Instagram sellers — from handmade crafts to supplements, pottery to fashion. Every seller is verified and reviewed by real buyers. Free sellers are available immediately. Subscribe to Anvaye Pass (₹50 / 15 days) to unlock all sellers.
Only real buyers can leave reviews
3
Browse products & add to cart
Explore each seller's full product catalogue. View detailed descriptions, pricing, ratings, and verified buyer reviews before you decide.
Full product details before you buy
4
Pay directly via UPI
No middlemen. Payment goes straight to the seller's UPI ID via Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. No holding periods, no delays. Instant and secure.
Secured by Razorpay
5
Track your order in real-time
Follow your order from confirmation to delivery. Get notified at every step — confirmed, packing, shipped, and delivered. No more wondering where your order is.
Real-time status updates
6
Leave a verified review
After delivery, share your experience. Only buyers who actually purchased can leave reviews — so every rating you see is genuine and tamper-proof. Your review helps other buyers make better decisions.
Verified purchase reviews only

For Sellers
From sign up to your first sale — here's exactly how it works
1
Verify your phone number
Quick OTP verification with your Indian mobile number. No lengthy KYC, no document uploads to get started. You can be live in under 5 minutes.
Live in under 5 minutes
2
Connect your Instagram handle
Your Instagram username becomes your store identity on Anvaye. Buyers who already follow you on Instagram can find your Anvaye store directly — no need to rebuild your audience.
Your existing audience finds you
3
List your products
Add products with photos, descriptions, and prices. Takes less than 2 minutes per product. No technical skills needed — if you can use Instagram, you can list on Anvaye.
2 minutes per product
4
Receive orders & get paid instantly
When a buyer places an order, payment goes directly to your UPI ID via Razorpay. Anvaye charges only 3% per successful sale — no monthly fees, no listing fees, no hidden charges. We only earn when you earn.
Only 3% per sale — no monthly fees
5
Manage orders from your dashboard
View all incoming orders in your seller dashboard. Update order status (Packing → Shipped → Delivered) and keep buyers informed automatically. See your revenue, ratings, and analytics at a glance.
All orders in one place

Build your reputation
After delivery, buyers can leave verified reviews. Only real buyers who purchased can review — so your rating is 100% genuine and tamper-proof. A strong rating means more trust, more buyers, and more sales.

Quick answers

Common questions

Is Anvaye free to use as a buyer?
Yes! Browsing and ordering from free sellers is completely free. Subscribe to Anvaye Pass (₹50 / 15 days) to unlock all sellers with no per-order fees.
How does Anvaye make money?
Anvaye charges sellers a flat 3% commission per successful sale. Buyers can also subscribe to Anvaye Pass for ₹50 / 15 days. No hidden fees anywhere.
Is my payment secure?
All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. Your card and UPI details are never stored on Anvaye servers.
What if I have a problem with my order?
Contact the seller directly through Anvaye's messaging system. If unresolved, our support team steps in within 24 hours to help mediate.
Can I sell on Anvaye without Instagram?
Currently Anvaye is designed for Instagram sellers. Your Instagram handle is your store identity. Support for other platforms is coming soon.
How quickly do I get paid as a seller?
Payments go directly to your UPI ID at the time of purchase. There is no holding period — money is in your account within minutes of the buyer paying.
